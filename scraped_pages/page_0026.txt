Faculty of computer science - OvGU Magdeburg FIN Officials
FIN Officials
If you are a student at the Computer Science faculty, or you have an acceptance letter and want to pursue a study course with us, this page is for you. In this section, we are listing various resources (websites, contacts, offices) that assist you before and during your stay here at OVGU.
NOTE: If you have any questions about a specific module, check LSF and the course website first. If your question is more general, check the corresponding website. If the websites do not answer your question, you can drop an email to the professor, lecturer or other contact person. Please make sure that your email is unambiguous and clearly states your requirement. [
