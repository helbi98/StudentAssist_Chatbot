Research at the Faculty of Computer Science
Research at our faculty is done to get more and more scientific knowledge in the area of computer science, to develop methods, tools and new application areas. Research is oriented at the sharp end of science.
The faculty is involved in a lot of interdisciplinary research projects at our university. More information about these projects you can find on the web-pages of our
departments
and their research groups.
Currently the focuses lie on scientific questions in the areas of Data and Knowledge Engineering and Computational Visualistics.
