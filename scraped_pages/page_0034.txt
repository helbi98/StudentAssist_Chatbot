Faculty of computer science - OvGU Magdeburg International Mentors
International Mentors
For new students, the international mentors serve as the first point of contact, helping them settle in and adapt to their new environment.
The mentors are a dedicated team of international students from the Faculty of Computer Science (FIN), committed to guiding and supporting fellow students throughout their academic journey and everyday life in Germany.
For reaching out to us send an email to:
fin-mentors@isg.cs.ovgu.de
